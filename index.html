FAQ

What is the current status of Herbalist?
So far, the basic Android client is work in progress. Currently, the database update from our Herbalist server is being implemented.
What are the killer features?
The app can filter the herb database by various keys like flower color or leaf shape. It can also navigate to previously stored places where certain herbs grow. The database is stored for offline usage as there may be some connection problems in terrain.
When Herbalist will be ready?
It depends on your support. The app self should be available later this year. The database will be filled step by step, based on available resources.
